Neutron

Design to be perform

Get to know Neutron

What Is Neutron?

Neutron is Atomo Innovation’s entry-level smart home controller - simple, efficient, and built for everyday homes.

Why It Exists?

To make smart living accessible to everyone by offering essential automation at an affordable price.

Who It's For?

Perfect for first-time users, renters, compact homes, and those who want plug-and-play automation.

Designed for Daily Comfort.

Handles daily tasks like lighting, fan control, remote switching, and basic sensor automation with ease.

Intuitive by Nature

Simple app interface and easy setup ensure users of all ages can operate Neutron without technical skills.

Works Quietly in the Background.

Neutron blends into your lifestyle - no fuss, just smart control that runs smoothly.

Smart Enough, Without the Complexity

Offers just the right amount of intelligence to automate your home without being overwhelming.

Future-Ready Starter Kit

A gateway into the Atomo ecosystem - start with Neutron and upgrade to Proton or Electron as your needs grow.

Affordable Doesn’t Mean Basic

Despite its price, Neutron supports key protocols like WiFi, BLE, RF, and IR.

Built in India for Indian Homes

Tailored to Indian power systems, appliance types, and lifestyles - proudly made for local needs.

Why Neutron is the best of device in the world

Essential Smart Home Hub

Neutron delivers all the core automation features needed for a modern smart home — simple, reliable, and affordable.

Multi-Protocol Support

Built-in support for WiFi, BLE, IR, RF, and basic compatibility for mainstream smart devices.

App-Based Control

Intuitive mobile app for controlling lights, fans, switches, and sensors from anywhere.

Seamless Device Pairing

Quick and user-friendly onboarding of smart devices with minimal setup.

Basic Automation Engine

Create schedules, timer-based triggers, and basic rule-based automations (e.g., motion → light ON).

Local + Cloud Sync

Runs key automations locally for speed and fallback, while syncing to the cloud for remote access and backup.

DIY & Installer Friendly

Ideal for first-time users, renters, or simple retrofits — no deep tech knowledge required.

Modular Expansion Ready

Supports add-ons for extending control to IR remotes, basic energy meters, and more.

Integrates with Proton & Electron

Functions independently or as part of a larger Atomo ecosystem - scales as your needs grow./p>
